Big Al, the organized crime boss, is living a life full of drugs, wealth, and guns, and is engaged in a triangle of love that is bringing his organization through the hardest time to date. Practice tests its chances and leadership, which can not fail in the face of dangerous threats, proving that when it comes to street code law, a relationship is a must.
